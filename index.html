ABOUT ME

I’m based in the New York/New Jersey area, but place is irrelevant to the type of work I love to do, which is to take complicated information from medical studies, distill it, and beautifully present it as a graphic that the reader can absorb quickly.

I have over a decade’s worth of experience in art and journalism and am a amateur photographer and dancer on the side.

My writing and editing experience has trained me to fully investigate the topic at hand and to communicate it with brevity and clarity.

Finally, not only is place irrelevant, so is topic. Please feel free to contact me if you work outside the health and medical fields. I love a challenge.
